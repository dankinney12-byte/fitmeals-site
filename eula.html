18. Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

18.1 Subscription Services

The Licensed Application may offer premium content, features, or services ("Premium Subscriptions") available on an auto-renewing subscription basis. Available subscription plans, including the subscription term (monthly or annual), will be displayed within the Licensed Application at the time of purchase.

18.2 Billing and Payment

Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account (for iOS) or your Google Play account (for Android) at confirmation of purchase. The price of the subscription will be shown at the time of purchase and may vary by plan, promotions, applicable taxes, and your region/currency as determined by the applicable Service.

18.3 Auto-Renewal

Premium Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current subscription period at the then-current price for your selected plan.

18.4 Management and Cancellation

You can manage your subscription and turn off auto-renewal at any time through your account settings in the applicable Service (Apple ID settings for iOS; Google Play subscriptions for Android). Canceling stops future renewals; you will continue to have access to Premium Subscriptions until the end of the current, already-paid subscription period.

18.5 Free Trials

If a free trial is offered, any unused portion of the trial period will be forfeited when you purchase a Premium Subscription, where applicable.
