FAQs

What is Fit Meals?

Fit Meals is a mobile app for Android and iOS that helps you find restaurant meals that match your macro goals. Set preferences like calories and protein, then browse restaurants to see meals that fit.

How do I set my macro preferences?

Open the app, go to your preferences page, and enter your targets (for example: minimum protein and maximum calories). These targets are for single meals, not your whole day. You can adjust these anytime.

Do I need an account to use Fit Meals?

Yes — an account helps sync preferences and saved meals across devices.

Where does the nutrition information come from?

Nutrition values come from restaurant-published nutrition information and structured meal entries in the app.

Are macros always exact?

Not always. Nutrition can vary by location, portioning, and preparation. Fit Meals provides high-quality estimates to help you decide faster, but you should confirm details with the restaurant if you need precise tracking.

Why don't I see a restaurant I expected?

Common reasons: the restaurant isn't within your search area, it's categorized differently in Maps/Places data, it's temporarily closed, or it doesn't have meals that match your current macro filters.

How do I save meals?

Tap on a meal card to bring up the full meal card. Tap the green save icon in the upper right corner. Saved items are available under your saved/favorites section.
